# MedicalLinks Ś Revised Product Roadmap
## Business Model Shift: Hospital-Access Talent Platform

## Product Direction Update

MedicalLinks has shifted from a recruiter-led workflow tool to a hospital-access talent platform.

Primary roles are now:
- **Doctor**
- **Hospital User**
- **Admin**

Recruiter is no longer a primary product role for the current MVP and should be deprioritized or removed from user-facing workflows unless explicitly needed for internal support later.

Hospitals should be able to:
- access a qualified doctor pool
- search and filter candidates
- shortlist candidates
- message candidates
- invite candidates to interview
- mark successful hires

Doctors should be able to:
- create a professional profile
- control visibility
- be discovered by hospitals
- respond to hospital outreach
- manage interviews and opportunities

Admins should be able to:
- approve hospitals
- manage commercial agreements
- monitor platform activity
- resolve disputes
- track hires and fee attribution

---

## Core Product Promise

MedicalLinks is a GCC-focused doctor discovery and hiring platform.

Hospitals:
- sign agreements
- receive controlled access to the doctor pool
- browse and filter candidates
- contact candidates
- interview candidates
- pay a success fee upon hire

Doctors:
- create one structured profile
- get discovered by GCC hospitals
- manage visibility and readiness
- engage directly with hospital hiring teams

## Revised MVP Priorities

## Phase 1 Ś Platform Reset

### Chunk 1 Ś Role and Access Model Reset
- doctor / hospital / admin become the primary roles
- recruiter is removed or hidden from user-facing flows
- route guards, navigation, and dashboards are updated

### Chunk 2 Ś Hospital Agreement and Account Activation
- hospital approval workflow
- hospital account status:
  - pending
  - active
  - suspended
  - expired
- agreement metadata:
  - commission percentage
  - start/end date
  - notes
  - service access status

### Chunk 3 Ś Doctor Privacy and Visibility Controls
- profile visibility settings
- open-to-opportunities status
- hide personal contact details by default
- hospital-visible candidate preview vs full profile visibility
- country / market visibility preferences

---

## Phase 2 Ś Hospital-Side Product Core

### Chunk 4 Ś Hospital Dashboard
- hospital home dashboard
- saved candidates
- recent activity
- upcoming interviews
- active opportunities
- quick actions

### Chunk 5 Ś Candidate Pool Browsing and Search
- searchable doctor directory
- filters by:
  - specialty
  - subspecialty
  - experience
  - country
  - language
  - GCC licensing readiness
  - relocation readiness
  - visa sponsorship need
  - availability
  - expected compensation

### Chunk 6 Ś Hospital-Facing Doctor Profile View
- doctor summary
- specialty and experience
- credentials/readiness summary
- location/preferences
- match indicators
- limited visibility rules for private info

### Chunk 7 Ś Shortlist and Candidate Pipeline
- save/favorite candidate
- shortlist page
- shortlist stages:
  - saved
  - reviewing
  - interview invited
  - interview completed
  - offer made
  - hired
  - rejected
- hospital notes/tags

### Chunk 8 Ś Hospital-to-Doctor Messaging
- hospital user can message doctor
- thread view
- unread/read handling
- candidate-linked conversation context

### Chunk 9 Ś Interview Invitation Workflow
- hospital invites doctor to interview
- doctor accepts/declines/reschedules
- interview details:
  - date/time
  - timezone
  - virtual/in-person
  - notes
- interview status tracking

## Phase 3 Ś Revenue and Commercial Workflow

### Chunk 10 Ś Hire Attribution and Placement Tracking

- connect hire to opportunity or direct discovery path

  - monthly salary
  - commission percentage
  - fee amount

### Chunk 11 Ś Commercial Records and Invoice Tracking
- fee/invoice record
- invoice status:
  - draft

- admin notes

### Chunk 13 Ś Dashboard and Navigation Rework

For hospitals:

For doctors:
- create one profile

## Fastest Path to a Usable Business MVP

If speed matters, prioritize these first:

1. Role and access model reset
2. Hospital agreement and account activation
3. Doctor privacy and visibility controls
4. Candidate pool search and filtering
5. Hospital-facing doctor profile view
6. Shortlist and candidate pipeline
7. Hospital-to-doctor messaging
8. Interview invitation workflow
9. Hire attribution and placement tracking

This is the shortest path to:
- hospital signs agreement
- hospital gets access
- hospital finds doctors
- hospital contacts them
- hospital interviews them
- you track the successful hire and fee

## Product Principles for All Future Chunks

- prioritize hospital self-service
- preserve doctor trust and privacy
- keep admin as oversight and commercial control
- use structured medical recruitment terminology
- avoid recruiter-led assumptions unless explicitly needed
- keep the platform practical and high-trust
- support GCC physician hiring realities
